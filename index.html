Questions people ask

Frequently asked.

What exactly do I get for $350?

A full ad slot on a 6"×11" two-sided postcard. We design your ad, help you write an offer that actually converts, add a trackable QR code, print it on thick glossy stock, and deliver it to 2,500 Killearn homes. Plus: industry exclusivity, a 30-day scan report, a photo of your card in a real mailbox, and a high-res digital copy for your own socials.

What happens if the card doesn't fill?

If we don't hit ten paid sponsors within 30 days, every sponsor gets their full $350 back. No forms. No store credit. No fees. Your money sits untouched with us until ten local businesses are on the card — if we don't get there, you walk away whole.

Who else is on the card besides me?

Up to thirteen other non-competing Tallahassee businesses. Think: the plumber next to the landscaper next to the roofer next to the family dentist. No direct competitors — that's the whole point. Once the card fills, we send every sponsor a short intro list so you know who else is on it.

Can I see the card before it prints?

Yes. You get a digital proof of your ad and a mockup of the full card layout before anything goes to the printer. You get one free revision. Nothing prints without your written approval.

Do I really get to lock out my competitors?

Yes. One business per industry per card. If you're a roofer and you claim the roofing slot, no other roofer appears on the same mailer to those 2,500 homes. Period.

My industry isn't shown in the slot grid — can I still buy?

Yes. The grid shows example trades; any non-competing business qualifies. Florist, attorney, family dentist, med spa, gym, tutor, insurance agent, boutique, chiropractor, real estate — if nobody in your exact category has claimed the card yet, you're in. Text us your trade and we'll confirm on the spot.

How do I pay?

Zelle to 954-614-5352. That's it. No Stripe, no invoices, no service fees. Once you send, we confirm, and your slot is locked.

When will my card actually hit mailboxes?

About three weeks after the card hits the 10-sponsor floor. Roughly one week for design and proofs, one week at the printer, one week for delivery to your mailbox.

What if I don't have a designed logo or a good offer?

We'll help you write the offer (e.g. "Free roof inspection — mention this card"), and if you don't have a logo we'll typeset your business name in a matching editorial style. You're not on your own.

Can I buy two slots for more space?

Yes — double slots are $600 (saves you $100 vs two singles) and give you 2× the ad area. Good fit for brands that want visual dominance on the card or want to feature two services.

Why Killearn Estates first?

Older homes, mature lawns, owner-occupied. That profile is the sweet spot for the services on this card — landscapers, HVAC techs, roofers, pool guys. It's where a $350 ad slot actually pays back. Future cards will hit SouthWood, Golden Eagle, and other high-income Tallahassee zips.
